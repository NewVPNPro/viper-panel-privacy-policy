Viper Panel Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Viper Panel works, what information may be used, and how we protect user data while providing access to our server management system.

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Viper Panel is an app that allows users to access and use our server through our panel management system. This Privacy Policy is made to explain in a simple and professional way how user information is handled.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: such as username, email address, or password used to access the app.
  • Server Configuration: information needed to connect users to our server.
  • Basic Device Information: such as device model, operating system, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting.
  • Connection Data: limited technical data required to maintain service performance and security.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To connect users to our server through the panel system.
  • To maintain app security, performance, and reliability.
  • To improve the service and fix technical issues.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user personal information. Data may only be shared when required by law, for security reasons, or when necessary to operate the service.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

6. User Choices

  • Users may stop using the app at any time.
  • Users may request account changes or deletion if supported by the service.
  • Users may uninstall the app to remove local app data stored on their device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Viper Panel is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
