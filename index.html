Viper Panel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

Introduction

Viper Panel allows you to access and manage your own servers through a panel management system. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. It is designed to meet Google Play’s User Data and Data Safety requirements.

Information We Collect

  • Credentials you provide: usernames, passwords, tokens, or API keys needed to sign in to your servers or panels.
  • Server/panel configurations: host addresses, ports, profile names, and settings you add to operate your infrastructure.
  • Diagnostics (optional/limited): crash logs and basic performance data to keep the app stable. No browsing history or precise location is collected.
  • Device info (minimal): device model, OS version, and network status only to troubleshoot connectivity.

We do not collect your name, contact list, photos, or other sensitive categories, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate you to the servers and panels you configure.
  • Display and execute panel functions you request.
  • Maintain security, performance, and reliability of the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations if required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except:

  • When you connect to services or APIs you configure (data goes directly to those endpoints).
  • When required by law or to protect user safety.
  • With service providers under confidentiality (e.g., crash analytics) strictly to support the app.

Data Storage & Retention

Credentials and configurations are stored locally on your device and remain until you delete them or uninstall the app. Diagnostic data, if sent, is retained only as long as needed to resolve issues and then deleted or anonymized.

Security

We use encrypted transport (e.g., HTTPS) where supported and apply reasonable safeguards to protect stored data. No method is 100% secure; please keep your device and credentials protected.

Your Choices

  • Edit or delete saved servers/accounts inside the app.
  • Decline or disable optional diagnostics (if offered).
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data.
  • Contact us for access, correction, or deletion requests.

Children

This app is intended for users 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to delete it.

International Transfers

Data is primarily stored on your device. If diagnostics or support data is processed on our systems, it may be handled in the United States or other locations consistent with this policy and applicable law.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date will change when we do. Material updates will be highlighted in-app or on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us at viperpanel2026@gmail.com.

Google Play Compliance

Viper Panel aligns with Google Play’s User Data, Data Safety, and Families policies. The Data Safety form will reflect the collection, use, and sharing described above, and the app honors user choices provided by the Play Console declarations.
